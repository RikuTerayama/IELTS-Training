# Writing/Speaking Note

## Task 1 - Graph

### Introduction phrases
The graph shows, The chart illustrates, The diagram presents, The table displays

### Overview phrases
Overall, In general, Generally speaking, On the whole

### Comparison phrases
In contrast, By contrast, Conversely, On the other hand, Similarly, Likewise

### Trend phrases
Increased, Decreased, Rose, Fell, Remained stable, Fluctuated

### Numbers
A third, One third, A quarter, One quarter, A half, Approximately, Roughly, About

## Task 1 - Diagram

### Process phrases
First, Second, Third, Finally, Initially, Subsequently, Eventually

### Connection phrases
Then, After that, Next, Meanwhile, At the same time

## Task 2 - Discussion

### Opinion phrases
In my opinion, I believe, I think, From my perspective, It seems to me

### Argument phrases
On the one hand, On the other hand, However, Nevertheless, Moreover, Furthermore

### Conclusion phrases
In conclusion, To sum up, In summary, To conclude

## Speaking - Cohesion

### Linking words
And, But, So, Because, Although, While, Since

### Fillers
Well, Actually, I mean, You know, Let me see

### Opinion starters
I think, I believe, In my view, From my point of view
