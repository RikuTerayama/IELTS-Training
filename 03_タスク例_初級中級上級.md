# タスク例：初級・中級・上級

## 初級タスク例

### お題
**Task 2: Some people think that working from home has more advantages than working in an office. Do you agree or disagree?**

Write at least 250 words.

---

### アプリが提示する出力

#### PREPガイド
**PREP構造とは:**
- **P (Point)**: 自分の意見を明確に述べる
- **R (Reason)**: 理由を2〜3つ挙げる
- **E (Example)**: 具体例を1つ挙げる
- **P (Point again)**: 結論で意見を再確認

**今回のタスクでの構成案:**
1. Introduction: 意見を述べる（例: "I agree that working from home has more advantages."）
2. Body Paragraph 1: 理由1（例: 時間の節約）
3. Body Paragraph 2: 理由2（例: ワークライフバランス）
4. Conclusion: 意見を再確認

#### 必須語彙（3〜5語）
- **commute** (v/n) - 通勤する/通勤
- **flexible** (adj) - 柔軟な
- **productivity** (n) - 生産性
- **work-life balance** (n) - ワークライフバランス
- **distraction** (n) - 気が散ること

---

### ユーザー入力（想定）

#### ステップ1: 日本語で回答作成
```
私は在宅勤務の方が良いと思います。

理由は3つあります。まず、通勤時間がなくなるので、その時間を仕事や趣味に使えます。次に、柔軟なスケジュールで働けるので、家族との時間も増えます。最後に、オフィスの雑音がないので、集中して仕事ができます。

例えば、私の友人は在宅勤務になってから、毎日1時間の運動時間ができて、健康になりました。

結論として、在宅勤務はオフィス勤務よりも多くの利点があると思います。
```

#### ステップ2: 英語骨格提示（アプリが生成）
```
I agree that working from home has more advantages than working in an office.

First, [穴埋め: 通勤時間がないことの利点]. This allows people to [穴埋め: 時間の使い方].

Second, [穴埋め: 柔軟なスケジュールの利点]. As a result, [穴埋め: ワークライフバランスの改善].

Finally, [穴埋め: オフィスの雑音がないことの利点]. This helps people [穴埋め: 集中力の向上].

For example, my friend [穴埋め: 友人の具体例].

In conclusion, working from home offers more benefits than working in an office.
```

#### ステップ3: 穴埋め回答（ユーザーが記入）
```
I agree that working from home has more advantages than working in an office.

First, people do not need to commute to work. This allows people to use that time for work or hobbies.

Second, people can work with a flexible schedule. As a result, they can spend more time with their families.

Finally, there are no distractions from the office. This helps people focus on their work better.

For example, my friend started working from home and now has one hour for exercise every day, which made him healthier.

In conclusion, working from home offers more benefits than working in an office.
```

### 穴埋め問題（弱点別）

#### CC (Coherence and Cohesion) 問題
**問題1**: 以下の文を適切な接続詞でつなげなさい。

"People do not need to commute. [    ] they can save time."

選択肢:
- A) However
- B) Therefore
- C) Although
- D) Meanwhile

**正解**: B) Therefore

**問題2**: 以下の段落に適切な接続表現を追加しなさい。

"First, people do not need to commute to work. [    ], people can work with a flexible schedule."

選択肢:
- A) In addition
- B) On the other hand
- C) In contrast
- D) For instance

**正解**: A) In addition

## 中級タスク例

### お題
**Task 2: Some people believe that university education should be free for all students, while others think that students should pay for their own education. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**

Write at least 250 words.

---

### アプリが提示する出力

#### PREPガイド
**今回のタスクでの構成案（Discuss both views）:**
1. Introduction: 両方の意見を紹介
2. Body Paragraph 1: 無料教育のメリット（理由2つ）
3. Body Paragraph 2: 有料教育のメリット（理由2つ）
4. Body Paragraph 3: 自分の意見と理由
5. Conclusion: 意見をまとめる

#### 必須語彙（3〜5語）
- **tuition fee** (n) - 授業料
- **accessibility** (n) - アクセシビリティ、利用しやすさ
- **financial burden** (n) - 経済的負担
- **merit-based** (adj) - 実力主義の
- **investment** (n) - 投資

---

### ユーザー入力（想定）

#### ステップ1: 英語でPREP回答
```
There are different opinions about whether university education should be free or paid.

Some people believe that free education increases accessibility. This means that students from poor families can study without worrying about money. Also, free education helps society because more people can get higher education.

However, others think that students should pay for their education. They argue that tuition fees make students study harder because they value their education more. In addition, universities need money to provide good facilities and teachers.

In my opinion, I think a balanced approach is better. The government should support students who cannot afford tuition, but students who can pay should contribute. This way, education remains accessible while maintaining quality.

In conclusion, both free and paid education have advantages, but a mixed system might be the best solution.
```

#### ステップ2: 穴埋め回答（アプリが生成した骨格に記入）
※ 中級では、ユーザーが書いた回答から特定の語彙を抜き出して穴埋め問題を生成

**穴埋め問題例:**
"Some people believe that free education increases [    ]. This means that students from [    ] families can study without worrying about [    ]."

選択肢:
- A) accessibility / poor / money
- B) availability / rich / time
- C) affordability / middle / tuition

**正解**: A

選択肢:

- B) bad
- C) weak

**正解**: A) low-income

**問題2**: 以下のコロケーションで正しいものを選びなさい。

"pay [    ] tuition"

選択肢:
- A) for
- B) to
- C) with
- D) by

**正解**: A) for

## 上級タスク例

### お題
**Task 2: Some people argue that the increasing use of artificial intelligence in the workplace will lead to widespread unemployment. Others believe that AI will create new job opportunities. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**

Write at least 250 words.

---

### アプリが提示する出力

#### 必須語彙（3〜5語）
- **automation** (n) - 自動化
- **displacement** (n) - 置き換え、代替
- **workforce** (n) - 労働力
- **augment** (v) - 強化する、増強する
- **paradigm shift** (n) - パラダイムシフト

※ 上級ではPREPガイドは表示しない（ユーザーが自分で構成を考える）

---

### ユーザー入力（想定）

#### 英語で回答（自由記述）
```
The rapid advancement of artificial intelligence has sparked a debate about its impact on employment. While some predict mass unemployment, others envision a future where AI creates new opportunities. This essay will examine both perspectives before presenting a balanced view.

Proponents of the unemployment argument point to automation's ability to replace human workers in routine tasks. For instance, manufacturing and data entry jobs are increasingly performed by machines, leading to job displacement. Moreover, AI's capacity to analyze vast amounts of data could render certain analytical roles obsolete.

Conversely, advocates for AI's job-creating potential argue that technological advancement historically generates new industries. The rise of the internet, for example, eliminated some jobs but created countless others in digital marketing, app development, and cybersecurity. Similarly, AI is likely to augment human capabilities rather than replace them entirely, requiring workers to adapt and acquire new skills.

In my view, the reality lies somewhere in between. While certain jobs will undoubtedly disappear, AI will simultaneously create demand for roles that require human creativity, emotional intelligence, and ethical judgment. The key challenge is ensuring that the workforce can transition through retraining and education programs.

In conclusion, AI's impact on employment is complex and multifaceted. Rather than fearing displacement, society should focus on preparing workers for this paradigm shift through proactive policy and educational initiatives.
```

## 共通の設計ポイント

### 1. レベル別の難易度調整
- **初級**: 日本語→英語の段階的アプローチ、基本的な語彙
- **中級**: 英語でPREP構造、やや高度な語彙

- 各タスクで3〜5語の必須語彙を提示
- タスク内で使用を促す
- Speakingプロンプトにも含める
- 単語学習機能と連動

- 最大3つのBand-up actions（優先順位付き）
- 具体例（改善前→改善後）を必ず含める
- 次回タスクへの実践的な提案
- 全文書き換え禁止（指定範囲のみ）

- 弱点タグ（CC/LR/GRA）に応じた問題生成
- 初級/中級のみ（上級はスキップ可能）
